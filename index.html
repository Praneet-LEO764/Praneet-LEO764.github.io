What I work on

Research Areas

Exploring the algorithmic and structural properties of graphs, with a focus on tractability and hardness.

🕸️

Graph Algorithms

Algorithmic problems on graphs including identifying codes, vertex splitting, and graph modification problems.

⚙️

Parameterized Algorithms

FPT and XP algorithms for NP-hard problems, including biclique contraction (O*(4.02^k)) and identifying codes.

🧩

Approximation Algorithms

Near-optimal algorithms with provable guarantees, recently constructing XP approximations for fair division on graphs.

📐

Combinatorial Optimization

Scheduling, fair allocation, and pursuit games, linking structural graph properties to optimization hardness.
